Features

Everything you need to navigate campus

Our app combines advanced technology with user-friendly design to make campus navigation effortless.

Interactive Campus Map

Explore our detailed 3D map of all campus buildings including Tech Block, Admin Block, and Academic Block.

Real-time Positioning

GPS and indoor BLE beacon technology helps you locate yourself anywhere on campus, even inside buildings.

Voice-guided Navigation

Get turn-by-turn voice directions to your classroom, library, or any other campus location.

Schedule Integration

Sync with your class schedule and get automatic reminders and directions to your next class.

QR Code Navigation

Scan QR codes placed around campus for instant directions to key locations like labs and workshops.

Campus Alerts

Receive real-time notifications about room changes, campus events, and important announcements.
